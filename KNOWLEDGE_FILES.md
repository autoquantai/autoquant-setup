# Knowledge Files

## Durable Placement

- `IDENTITY.md`: persona, role, repo URL, tone, durable traits
- `AGENTS.md`: operating policy and execution rules
- `TOOLS.md`: command and tooling behavior
- `USER.md`: stable user preferences
- `SOUL.md`: mission and values
- `TRANSFERRABLE_KNOWLEDGE.md`: long-term research findings tagged with `[run_id]`

Do not place core operating guidance into `HEARTBEAT.md`, `BOOTSTRAP.md`, or `MEMORY.md`.

## Practice

- Save only durable guidance in durable files.
- Keep run-specific notes tied to the relevant `run_id`.
- Promote a lesson to durable knowledge only when it transfers across runs.
